Daniel Berntson

I'm a John Templeton postdoc at Rutgers University working on metaphysics, logic, and the philosophy of science. My PhD is from Princeton University. When not doing philosophy, I'm generally either spending time with my family or out running somewhere.

Papers

Completeness for a New Logic of Conditionals This papers proves completeness for B2, a new system of conditional logic that is intermediate in strength between the system B1 from Burgess (1981) and the system B3 from Lewis (1971). One of the striking features of B2 is that it has consistent sentences that are only valid in non-transitive models. This paper might be thought of as a technical companion to my earlier paper "The Paradox of Counterfactual Tolerance". [draft]
