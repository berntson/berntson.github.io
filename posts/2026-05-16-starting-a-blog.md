This is a small corner of the internet where I plan to send things when
they feel done.

I keep a lot of notes in Obsidian — half-formed ideas, drafts, reactions
to papers I am reading. Most of them stay private, and that is fine.
But some of them want to be finished. They want a destination, even a
quiet one. This blog is that destination.

The bar is not "publishable" in the academic sense. The bar is "I am
willing to put my name on it." If a post helps me think a little more
clearly, and if a stranger or a friend stumbles across it and finds it
worth their time, that is enough.

Topics will likely cluster around things I already think about: logic,
counterfactuals, the philosophy of science, occasional public-philosophy
questions. There will probably also be detours into tools, workflows,
and whatever I am reading.

If you want to follow along, there is an [Atom feed](../feed.xml).
