Daniel Berntson

The Counterfactual Logic of Interval Orders and Semiorders

Draft, work in progress.

Counterfactuals are often modeled using comparative relations on possible worlds. Such relations include total orders (Stalnaker), total preorders (Lewis), and preorders (Pollock, Kratzer). In this paper, I extend this research program by introducing new systems of counterfactual logic based on interval orders and semiorders. I then show that all the new systems are weakly complete and decidable, and that many are strongly complete, and therefore also compact.
